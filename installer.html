Overview

GPGTools Installer is an open source installer for Apple OS X. It installs all related OpenPGP applications, plugins and dependencies. You can choose which components to install.

Support and Issues

Please have a look at the list of open issues and ask the mailing list for further support.

Often reported limitations:

  • Issue #36: System wide installation of GPGMail not possible, yet.

Main Features

  • Integrated MacGPG2 (does not break your gpg1 installation, if there is one).
  • Integrated GPGMail.
  • Integrated GPG Keychain Access.
  • Integrated GPGServices.
  • Integrated GPGPreferences (initial version, GPG and GPGMail auto-fix only).
  • Integrated Enigmail.
  • The application is 64-bit and Snow Leopard ready.
