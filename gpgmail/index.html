Overview

GPGMail is an open source plugin for Apple Mail. It brings the functionality to sign, verify, encrypt and decrypt mails using the OpenPGP standard.

Please have a look at the getting started and FAQ section. It would be nice to tell Apple to support bundles for Mail.app officially.



Support and Issues

Please have a look at the list of open issues and ask the mailing list for further support. Additionally, we provide information in our wiki.

Often reported limitations:

  • Issue #94: E-Mails are empty.
  • Issue #11: Unread count goes out of sync.
  • Issue #17: Sometimes the message "E-Mail was encrypted but not PGP signed" prevents decryption.
  • Issue #107: Automatic update is not compatible with system wide installations (/L/M/B/).
  • Issue #135: Plugin is not compatible to OS X Lion, yet.

Main Features

  • Is part of the GPGTools Installer.
  • Sign, encrypt, decrypt, and verify all your mails.
  • Integrated update mechanism and installer can auto-fix the current installation after an OS update.
  • The bundle is 64-bit and Snow Leopard ready.
